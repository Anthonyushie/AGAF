Inspiring a new generation of African leaders

we are aglow aid foundation and Our mission is to empower and inspire a new generation of African leaders by giving them the knowledge and resources they need to improve themselves, their organizations, and their communities.

We Empower, We Inspire, and We Enlighten......

We Empower

We empower young people by providing them with platforms and chances to contribute to community development and peace-building efforts.

We Inspire

Young Africans are enlightened by our efforts to instill values-based leadership in them, and we encourage them to take action to better their communities and the lives of those around them.

We Enlighten

It is our mission to empower children with the tools they need to realize their full potential in the workplace and in the community, as well as the knowledge and skills needed to succeed as entrepreneurs.
