About Us

WHO ARE WE?

Aglow Aid Foundation (AGAF) is a social enterprise and a non-governmental organization that is committed to improving the lives of young people through the development of leadership, education, and entrepreneurship skills to bridge the gap between those who have the opportunity and those who don't. As a mission-driven organization, we understand that youth leadership and inclusion are critical to the development of a nation and the creation of wealth. Our innovative conceptual forces of change inspire and equip young people, transforming their mindsets and motivating them to effect positive change in their communities;

while also assisting social entrepreneurs in the development of systems and structures that are critical for business sustainability, thereby contributing to livelihoods, social and national development. As Nigeria's start-up youth-oriented non-profit organization, we are focused on making secondary education work for young people and developing talent to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Aglow Aid Foundation engages with local and international organizations, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), private and public educational institutions, healthcare centers, hospitals, faith-based and government agencies to support her projects and community development activities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to empower and inspire a new generation of African leaders by giving them the knowledge and resources they need to improve themselves, their organizations, and their communities..f

OUR VISION

We want to be known as the best resource center in Africa for training leaders who will help the continent realize its most significant potential.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • • Empower: We empower young people by providing them with platforms and chances to contribute to community development and peace-building efforts.
  • • Inspire: Young Africans are enlightened by our efforts to instill values-based leadership in them, and we encourage them to take action to better their communities and the lives of those around them.
  • • Enlighten: It is our mission to empower children with the tools they need to realize their full potential in the workplace and in the community, as well as the knowledge and skills needed to succeed as entrepreneurs.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • • Respect: As an organization, we are devoted to developing inclusive possibilities and working with all stakeholders with the greatest degree of professionalism.
  • • Integrity: In all of our dealings, we display honesty and openness.
  • • To promote young leadership development, we establish an atmosphere that encourages cooperation and strategic partnerships.
  • • Excellence: We are results-oriented, and inventive and go the additional mile to ensure that our programs and events meet or exceed the highest standards.
