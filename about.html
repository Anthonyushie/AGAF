About Us

a. WHO ARE WE?

Aglow Aid Foundation (AGAF) is a social enterprise and a non-governmental organization that is committed to improving the lives of young people through the development of leadership, education, and entrepreneurship skills to bridge the gap between those who have the opportunity and those who don't. As a mission-driven organization, we understand that youth leadership and inclusion are critical to the development of a nation and the creation of wealth. Our innovative conceptual forces of change inspire and equip young people, transforming their mindsets and motivating them to effect positive change in their communities; while also assisting social entrepreneurs in the development of systems and structures that are critical for business sustainability, thereby contributing to livelihoods, social and national development. As Nigeria's start-up youth-oriented non-profit organization, we are focused on making secondary education work for young people and developing talent to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Aglow Aid Foundation engages with local and international organizations, non-governmental organizations (NGOs), private and public educational institutions, healthcare centers, hospitals, faith-based and government agencies to support her projects and community development activities.
