Help to Humanity Over Self

"Help to Humanity Over Self" is a term that embodies the principle of altruism, whereby one prioritizes the wellbeing of others over their own needs and desires. It is a key concept in the field of philanthropy and social service, emphasizing the importance of empathy, compassion, and selflessness in serving communities and improving people's lives. The philosophy behind "Help to Humanity Over Self" is grounded in the recognition that we all share a common humanity, and that it is our duty to use our resources and abilities for the greater good of society. In practice, this means putting aside our personal interests and investing time, energy, and resources into helping others in need. This can take many forms, from volunteering at a local charity organization to donating money to a cause we believe in. At its core, "Help to Humanity Over Self" reflects a deep-seated commitment to service and social responsibility, echoing the words of Mahatma Gandhi, who famously said, "the best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others." Indeed, by helping others, we become more connected to our fellow human beings, and we gain a sense of purpose and fulfillment that is difficult to find through individual pursuits alone. In the context of Nigeria, where poverty and inequality remain pervasive challenges, the concept of "Help to Humanity Over Self" holds particular significance. Non-governmental organizations like Aglow Aid Foundation (AGAF) are playing a critical role in addressing these issues, by providing critical services such as healthcare, education, and economic empowerment to vulnerable populations. AGAF's commitment to "Help to Humanity Over Self" is evident in its mission, which states that they exist to "provide holistic and integrated care to underprivileged populations across Nigeria, with a primary focus on women, children, and people living with disabilities." Through its various programs and initiatives, AGAF is working to address some of the key drivers of poverty and inequality in the country, by providing access to essential services and support networks that can help individuals and communities lift themselves out of poverty. That said, we encourage you to partner with us on reaching as many people as possible and making our vision a reality.
