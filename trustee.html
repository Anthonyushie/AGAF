Our Trustee

Meet the team.

Mr John Umeojiako

John Umeojiako was born and raised in Onitsha Anambra State. He went to school at Dennis Memorial Grammar School. After which he completed an Associate Degree at Dallas College, Brookhaven Campus. His undergraduate was in Finance at Northeastern State University in Broken Arrow and he has a Masters in Cybersecurity from Western Governors University. Having been heavily involved in community engagement advocacy in Dallas TX when he was a student, John thought it wise to initiate an organization which will help a lot of diasporas to give back to Nigeria through investing in human beings and effecting a social change. That was how he founded Aglow Aid Foundation. John's goal is to help humanity over self by developing projects and initiating community engagement activities to alleviate poverty of the soul and mind. His goal is to change Nigeria, one man at a time. So, he and his team are determined to gather the resources needed from like minded individuals for these life changing projects.

Mrs. Chisom Umeojiako

Chisom Umeojiako (Nee Obineme), wife of the founder of Aglow Aid Foundation, was born and raised in Nawfia, Anambra state. She attended Holy Innocents Juniorate Convent, Nkpor for her secondary education. Chisom had her undergraduate studies in Anambra State University, Igbariam campus where she acquired a Bachelor's degree in Linguistics/Igbo language. There, she emerged as the best graduating student in her department as well as the faculty of Arts. Chisom's immeasurable love for humanity and undying passion to render help to the less-privileged found expression in this charitable foundation. It is her uttermost pleasure to be part of this ingenious team which has set out to impact lives positively with as many resources they can lay their hands on. She is a formidable team member with the burning desire to render help to humanity over self!

Barr. Lucky Ezealor

Lucky Ezealor, esq. is an experienced legal practitioner with demonstrated history of working in the law practice industry. He is a strong professional graduate of the Nigerian Law School, Yenegoa campus and was called to the Nigerian Bar in 2017. Since then, he has worked with notable law firms and lawyers in the likes of Mike Ozekhome’s Chambers, Ngozi Olehi and Co. and Pere Lex Chambers. A seasoned attorney skilled in Dispute Resolution, Legal Research, Public Speaking, Legal drafting, Property law, Corporate Law and Litigation. He is currently the Head of Chambers in Randle and Croft Chambers and the Principal Attorney in Ezealor & Associates.

Mr Victor Elendu

Victor Elendu holds a B.Eng in Chemical Engineering from the Federal University of Technology, Owerri, Nigeria. He is a creative content writer focused on business, finance, and technology. With over three years of experience, he creates content for marketing and educational purposes for brands, organizations and individuals. Victor is driven by a vision of spreading happiness through compassion and improving lives through empathy.He leverages his skills in writing to advance the Foundation's philanthropic mission.
