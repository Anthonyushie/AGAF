Our Trustee

Meet the team.

Mr John Umeojiako

I was born and raised in Onitsha Anambra State. I went to school at Dennis Memorial Grammar School. After which I completed Associate Degree at Dallas College, Brookhaven Campus. My undergraduate was in Finance at Northeastern State University in Broken Arrow and I have a Masters in Cybersecurity from Western Governors University. Having been heavily involved in community engagement advocacy in Dallas TX when I was a student, I thought it wise to initiate an organization which will help a lot of diasporas to give back to Nigeria through investing in human beings and effecting a social change. That was how we gave birth to Aglow Aid Foundation. Our goal is to help humanity over self by developing projects and initiating community engagement activities to alleviate poverty of the soul and mind. Our goal is to change Nigeria, one man at a time. So, my team and I are determined to gather the resources needed from like minded individuals for these life changing projects.

Mrs. Chisom Umeojiako

Chisom Umeojiako (Nee Obineme) wife of the founder, Aglow aid foundation. I was born and bred in Nawfia, Anambra state. I attended Holy Innocents Juniorate Convent, Nkpor for my secondary education. I had my undergraduate studies in Anambra state university, Igbariam campus where I acquired a Bachelors degree in Linguistics/ Igbo language. There, I emerged as the best graduated student in my department as well as the faculty of Arts. My immeasurable love for humanity and the undying passion to render help to the less-privileged found their way of expression in this charitable foundation. It is my uttermost pleasure to be part of this ingenious team which has set out to impact lives positively with as much resources they can lay their hands on. We are a formidable team with the burning desire to render help to humanity over self!.
