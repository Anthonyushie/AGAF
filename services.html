Our services

What We Do

Here's a summary of what we do......

Capacity Building and Education Training.

To fulfill our mission, AGAF provides environmental and renewable energy education to a targeted audience via workshops and seminars.

Mobilization and Advocacy.

AGAF works to enhance the lives of women, adolescents, and other marginalized populations by raising their awareness, educating them, and empowering them. Several conferences, seminars, and workshops have therefore been held, some in partnership with key government entities, to implement policy reforms on environmental and economic empowerment concerns.

Symposiums/Conferences.

AGAF addresses Gender and environmental issues during its yearly symposiums and workshops.

About Us

Aglow Aid Foundation (AGAF) is a social enterprise and a non-governmental organization that is committed to improving the lives of young people through the development of leadership, education, and entrepreneurship skills to bridge the gap between those who have the opportunity and those who don't.
